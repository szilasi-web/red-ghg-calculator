How it works

From feedstock to a report your auditor reads

The calculator does the heavy lifting. You enter what goes into the digester; it returns the certified-format numbers and the document.

1

Enter your feedstocks

Pick from 162 substrates and enter yearly tonnage. Transport, energy mix and process settings are pre-filled and editable.

2

Get the GHG intensity

Instant gCO₂eq/MJ — per feedstock and weighted — with the saving against the 94 gCO₂eq/MJ fossil comparator.

3

Generate the report

One click produces a branded, itemized RED III Annex VI report with applied factors, sources, watermark and unique ID.

4

Take it to your CB

Hand the report to your Certification Body. The tool computes; the CB audits and issues the binding Proof of Sustainability.

What you get

Built for the audit, not just a number

📊

Itemized Annex VI calculation

Every emission term (Eec, El, Ep, Etd, Eu, Esca, Eccs) laid out, with the Ep breakdown — slip, electricity, heat, compression, storage.

🧾

Applied factors & sources

The emission factors used, each with its basis — RED III Annex VI, ISCC EU 205, IPCC AR5 — so the methodology is transparent.

⚖️

Default & actual pathways

Pure manure or biowaste uses the RED III default value; mixtures fall back to the actual energy-share calculation automatically.

🔒

Licensed, non-transferable PDF

Each report carries a watermark, a unique report ID and a “prepared for [facility] — not transferable” licence line.

💶

Indicative offtake value

See the green premium, offtake price and annual revenue implied by your GHG intensity — a commercial read, alongside the compliance number.

⚡

Instant & up to date

Reflects current rules — including the 2026 compression requirement — and recalculates live as you change inputs.
